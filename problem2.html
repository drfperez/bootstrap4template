CARBON FOOTPRINT OF A CAR

This calculator uses actual constants and your information to calculate the level of emissions your car makes. This calculus not only uses the emission of the engine, but the emissions produced while making the car. For each 1000 pounds spent making a car, it's estimated that 750kg of CO2 have been emitted. Also we use the mean of KM travelled in a year, 15000km.
